AlectOS Presentations Page

A big part of sigops' goals of learning and teaching is presentations. We welcome presentations of all types, from the informal, unscheduled brain-dump, to the pre-planned powerpoint talk. This page is to share slides, notes, diagrams, etc of talks, as well as ideas for future talks if people are willing to take the initiative to give one.

Presentation ideas:
  • x86 architecture overview
  • x86 memory system
  • dealing with concurrency
  • makefiles, objdump, and linker scripts
  • VGA graphics
  • USB use, protocol, HW
  • x86 interrupt system
Presentations already given:
  • basic OS concepts (9/9/2003)
  • using cvs and doxygen (9/16/2003)
